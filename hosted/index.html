Marco Polo

  • Game Description
    This game is a replica of the classic game Marco Polo. Built with Materialize for the front end, NodeJs for the backend, Socket.io for websockets utility and the Canvas API for drawing
  • How to Play
    To start the game, all players must hit the ready button. Once the game starts, you will become the seeker or one of the many runners. If the only object spawned in the canvas is a red square, you are the seeker. Otherwise you are a runner.

    As a seeker... your job is to find and collide with all other players within the time limit. You will have 30 seconds to find them all. In order call out "Marco!" and see the other players, you have to hit the spacebar but be careful as you only have 6 location checks! Colliding with all players will win the game!

    As a runner... your job is to avoid the seeker at all costs! You can see all the other players in the game who will also be trying to avoid the seeker. If you can survive for 30 seconds you win!

  • Controls
    Movement: WASD

    Check Location: Spacebar
